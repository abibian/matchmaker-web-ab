Matchmaker Web

This program is designed to determine if you are compatible with me by asking five questions. These questions will provide a compatibility score after answering that will be calculated with respect with my desired responses from you. The closer the compatibility score is to 100, the more compatible you are with me. For each question, answer 5 means that you strongly agree with the question, answer 4 means that you agree with the question, 3 means that you don't agree but don't disagree, 2 means that you disagree, and 1 means that you strongly disagree.
